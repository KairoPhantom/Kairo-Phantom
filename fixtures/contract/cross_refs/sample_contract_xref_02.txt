MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement ("MSA") is entered into as of January 15, 2026, by and between Globex Corporation ("Provider") and Initech LLC ("Client").

1. Services:
Provider shall deliver the consulting services described in Exhibit B. The scope of each engagement shall be defined per the statement of work referenced in Section 3.

2. Payment Terms:
Client shall pay all invoices within 45 days of receipt. Late payments shall accrue interest at 1.5% per month as specified in Section 1 of Exhibit B.

3. Term and Renewal:
This MSA shall remain in effect for three (3) years from the Effective Date. Renewal terms are governed by Section 1 of this Agreement.

4. Limitation of Liability:
Provider's total liability shall not exceed the fees paid under Section 2 in the twelve months preceding the claim. The confidentiality obligations in Section 5 survive termination.

5. Confidentiality:
All confidential information shared under this MSA shall be protected per the terms defined in Section 2 of Exhibit B.

EXHIBIT B — Service Level Details
Service levels and response times are defined per the payment terms in Section 2. All service credits are calculated based on the limitation of liability in Section 4.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this MSA.
