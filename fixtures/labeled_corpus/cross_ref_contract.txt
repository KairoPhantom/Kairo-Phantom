MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

Section 1: Definitions
"Notice Period" means the period defined in Section 4.2.
"Confidential Information" has the meaning set forth in Section 3.

Section 3: Confidentiality
All Confidential Information shall be kept secret for five years.

Section 4: Termination
4.1 Either party may terminate with written notice.
4.2 The Notice Period shall be ninety (90) days.

Section 5: Governing Law
This agreement is governed by the laws of New York.
